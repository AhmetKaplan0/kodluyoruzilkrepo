__[22,27,16,2,18,6]__

1. _Verilen dizinin sort türüne göre aşamaları:_
  

   > n -> [16,22,27,2,18,6]

   > n1 -> [2,16,22,27,18,6]

   > n2 -> [2,16,18,22,27,6]

   > n3 -> [2,6,16,18,27,22]

    > O(n^2)

3. _Time Complexity:_

   > Best case: O(n) = O(6)

   > Worst case: O(n^2) = 0(36)    

   > Average case: O(n^2) = O(36)
